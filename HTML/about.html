About Us

Lets dive into the knowing’s of happenings or events that has made us come this long!!

When was the organisation developed?

Just from 3 members working with laptops in a room of rented house from 2016 to this 10-storeyed building our success hustles round a loop!

What was the purpose of developing this organisation?

Just when young minds with divulging ideas meet then they catch up the fire of rising above in quenching their dreams and helping others at least get their drop. A start-up which had been self-aided now stands as one of the leading organizations which feeds the hungry talents!!

How our organisation is helping the society?

With every penny that gets invested or is withdrawn we make sure that 0.5% of the amount is donated to the funds that we keep for various needy people. At the collection of a particular amount of money we always focus on different sufferers of the society. With each minimal amount we get, we make that use for the downtrodden.
